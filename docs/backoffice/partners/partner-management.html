Partner Management

Objective​

Partner Management allows viewing existing partners and onboarding new partners with correct configurations.

Add New Partner (Maker)​

Navigate to Backoffice → Partners → Partner Management → Add New Partner and fill:

Partner Information​

  • Partner Name (mandatory)
  • Country (mandatory)
  • Address (mandatory)

Integration Mode Settings​

For each mode (OTC, FT, IBFT), configure:

  • Sender Screening (toggle)
  • Receiver Screening (toggle)
  • Title Match (toggle)

Settlement and Contact​

  • Point of Contact (mandatory)
  • Email (mandatory)
  • Contact Number (mandatory)
  • BIPL Settlement Account (mandatory)
  • Fetch Title (toggle/setting)
  • Account Title (mandatory when title fetch applies)

Approval​

Partner onboarding must be approved before it becomes active.
