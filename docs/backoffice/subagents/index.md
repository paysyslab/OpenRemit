title: Sub-Agent Overview
---

## Objective
Sub-Agents represent operational entities under the remittance distribution network. This module supports onboarding and administration of sub-agents and their users.

## Key Capabilities
- Maintain sub-agent master data;
- Configure settlement details and title fetch where applicable;
- Manage sub-agent users and bulk user uploads;
- Use Maker/Checker for controlled onboarding and changes.
